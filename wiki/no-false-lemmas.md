# No False Lemmas

**Node Type:** thesis
**Node ID:** 0eb0dc70-2d66-4309-81ae-2926a74aa1d3
**Depth:** 1
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What is knowledge?](what-is-knowledge.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false assumptions**
