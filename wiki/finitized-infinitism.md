# Finitized Infinitism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 66a02180-9ca6-4520-acef-68ad69d2c1f2
**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is the concept of infinite justification compatible with the inherent limitations of human cognition?](is-the-concept-of-infinite-justification-compatible-with-the-inherent-limitations-of-human-cognition.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification for believing the proposition involves a potentially infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification involves a potentially infinite chain of reasons, which is revisable and extendable in principle**
