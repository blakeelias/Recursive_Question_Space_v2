# Is it possible to have an infinite series of reasons in finite time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6ea19f12-76c2-4bd2-825a-23e098837705
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism.md)

## Content

**Human cognition and memory are finite and limited resources.**, **In practical experience, reasoning and justification processes tend to conclude in a finite amount of time.**, **If justification requires an infinite series of reasons, it may be beyond human capacity to achieve knowledge.**
