# Are all reliable true beliefs virtuous?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b7046a4-9db4-40db-ae6d-e0005c36311c
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person consistently forms true beliefs by relying on a horoscope. Intuitively, we would judge that astrological methods do not constitute an intellectual virtue, even if they occasionally produce true beliefs.**, **It is implausible that mere reliability without a methodological basis implies intellectual virtue.**
