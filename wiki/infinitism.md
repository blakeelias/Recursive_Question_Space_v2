# Infinitism

**Node Type:** thesis
**Node ID:** 6ee1de27-0cbb-4d4a-917e-35f0d1bff526
**Depth:** 1
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What is knowledge?](what-is-knowledge.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the belief involves an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**
