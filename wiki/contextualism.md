# Contextualism

**Node Type:** thesis
**Node ID:** 742464b5-295a-45b3-955e-ea5e75dac911
**Depth:** 1
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What is knowledge?](what-is-knowledge.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for justification can vary depending on the context in which knowledge is claimed**
