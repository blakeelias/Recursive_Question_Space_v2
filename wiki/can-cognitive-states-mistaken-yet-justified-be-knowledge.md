# Can cognitive states mistaken yet justified be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1708341-0045-49a6-96c9-61e6507b2128
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalism](internalism.md)

## Content

**A person believes they are feeling cold and internally justifies this by perceiving what really are symptoms of fever as cold sensations.**, **Even if the thermometer outside shows a drop in temperature coinciding with their feelings, the mix of cognitive confusion persistently questions if belief amounts to knowledge.**
