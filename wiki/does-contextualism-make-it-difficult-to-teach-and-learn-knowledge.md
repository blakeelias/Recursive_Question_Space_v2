# Does contextualism make it difficult to teach and learn knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7742351a-31dd-41a8-8d5b-08c797065df6
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism.md)

## Content

**For effective teaching and learning, it is crucial to have clear criteria for what constitutes knowledge.**, **Different contexts might impose varying standards, confusing students about when someone truly knows something.**, **If students are taught varying standards depending on context, they might struggle to apply what they learn in different settings, thus complicating the learning process.**
