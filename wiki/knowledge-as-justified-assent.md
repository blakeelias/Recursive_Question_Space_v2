# Knowledge as Justified Assent

**Node ID:** 3698afbb-3c9d-4b27-b273-df8d8b611068

**Parent:** [How do justified true beliefs relate to this theory?](how-do-justified-true-beliefs-relate-to-this-theory.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they assent to the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their assent is justified**, **Justified assent, rather than unwavering certainty, can ground knowledge**
