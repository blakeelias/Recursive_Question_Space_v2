**Parent:** [Can coherence account for context-sensitive knowledge?](can-coherence-account-for-context-sensitive-knowledge.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's system of beliefs**, **knowledge requires that coherence is maintained across different contexts**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and coherent contextual stability is maintained**
