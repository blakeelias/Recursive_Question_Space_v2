# Can technology undermine the necessity of intellectual virtue for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 610bb7fc-2984-4698-89cb-1ef22bb63182
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** Yes

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology.md)

## Content
