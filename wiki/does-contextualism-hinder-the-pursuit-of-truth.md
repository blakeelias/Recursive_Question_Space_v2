# Does contextualism hinder the pursuit of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f52a1257-6543-4c44-9e12-127f09b1686b
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism.md)

## Content

**The pursuit of truth demands consistent criteria for determining what counts as justification, as truth is not supposed to vary by context.**, **If justification standards can vary with context, then the alignment of knowledge claims with truth may become difficult to ascertain, undermining the pursuit of objective truth.**
