**Parent:** [Does coherence allow for imbalanced belief weight?](does-coherence-allow-for-imbalanced-belief-weight.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres within a hierarchical system prioritizing foundational beliefs**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres within a hierarchical system prioritizing foundational beliefs**
