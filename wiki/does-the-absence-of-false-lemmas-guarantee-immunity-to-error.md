# Does the absence of false lemmas guarantee immunity to error?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e904880f-30e5-43a6-8664-41aa52b5208a
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas](no-false-lemmas.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where a person bases a belief on incomplete but true evidence, and their conclusion coincidentally aligns with undiscovered facts. Intuitively, there's hesitation to consider this belief as knowledge due to the shaky inference process.**, **A conclusion might be correct without reliance on false assumptions, but it can still be vulnerable to revision with gaining more complete information.**
