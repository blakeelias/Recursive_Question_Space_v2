# Is introspection enough for epistemic access?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c273d4f6-37ee-4425-ae23-3bddedff98de
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalism](internalism.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual is certain of a thoughtful decision based only on introspection, internally justifying their belief in an abstract philosophy.**, **Without consideration of empirical evidence from studies that challenge this philosophy, there is skepticism that such introspection alone makes for true knowledge.**
