# Can someone know something if their false beliefs are irrelevant?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 191f98c3-718a-4911-89a1-cb3947bd16b2
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas](no-false-lemmas.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a true proposition based on several different sources, with one source providing false information that does not affect the belief's truth. Intuitively, we might still regard this as knowledge.**, **When a false assumption does not contribute causally to the formation of a belief, intuitively, it seems plausible the belief could still constitute knowledge.**
