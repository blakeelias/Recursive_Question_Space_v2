# Does the requirement of infinite justification render knowledge impossible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 73b9ac5f-9218-422d-ba2e-9e46fbb3b786
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism.md)

## Content

**If knowledge requires an infinite chain of non-repeating reasons, then it becomes practically impossible for anyone to achieve knowledge.**, **The fact that people claim knowledge in daily life suggests that achieving infinite justification is not a necessary condition for knowledge.**
