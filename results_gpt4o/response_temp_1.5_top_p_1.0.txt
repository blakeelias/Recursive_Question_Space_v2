Knowledge as Justified True Belief:
- For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition
- For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition
- For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true

Indefeasibility of Justification:
- To know a proposition, a person must believe the proposition
- To know a proposition, a person must have indefeasible justification, meaning no future discoveries can undermine the justification
- To know a proposition, it is required that it is true

Reliabilism Theory of Knowledge:
- Knowledge entails that one's belief is produced by a process that reliably leads to truth
- For someone to know a proposition, they must believe the proposition
- For knowledge, the proposition must indeed be true
