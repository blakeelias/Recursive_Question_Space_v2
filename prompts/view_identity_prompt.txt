Two views that are mere paraphrases of each other also receive a score of 100. Two views can be semantically the same and receive a score of 100 even though the same commitments are merely stated in fewer propositions.

Two views are not at all the same if the truth or falsity of one is completely independent of the truth or falsity of the other, in which case the matching score is 0. Two views are also not at all the same if the truth of one of the views would make it impossible for the other view to be true, in which case the matching score should is also 0.
 
Two views can match somewhat if one of them is an elaboration of the other, but this would not be enough for an exact match.
